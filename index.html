      Set study load

      Domestic students can study 1, 2, 3, or 4 subjects each semester.

      International students must normally study a full load of 4 subjects (visa requirement). This can only be varied in exceptional circumstances such as a high failure rate that led to an academic intervention.

      Studying more than 4 subjects is only possible when 9 or fewer subjects remain in the final year or 5 or fewer subjects remain in the last semester.
